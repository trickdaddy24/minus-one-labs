# Minus One Labs

**Your business. Online in 14 days — website, reviews, and SEO included.**

Minus One Labs is a done-for-you website platform for local businesses. We handle everything: design, build, hosting, reviews, and SEO — so you can focus on running your business.

---

## What We Offer

- **One-page website** — About, Services, Contact form, and live visitor comments
- **Custom domain setup** — We handle registration and DNS
- **Hosting included** — Cloudflare Pages (global CDN, SSL, blazing fast)
- **Google review widget** — Display your ratings and automate review requests
- **SEO built in** — Schema markup, fast load times, local search optimization

---

## How It Works

1. **Request a quote** (free) — Create an account and submit your business info
2. **Receive a proposal** — We reply within 24 hours with pricing and timeline
3. **Accept → Questionnaire** — Fill out a 10-minute form about your site content and preferences
4. **Launch** — We build and go live within 14 days
